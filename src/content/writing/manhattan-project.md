Someone commented today that [Doug](https://astronomy.fas.harvard.edu/people/douglas-finkbeiner) described working in AI safety as "it's like working on the Manhattan Project." I thought it was a good way of capturing how things are going and likely not far off from the truth. Maybe we should've taken Aschenbrenner's [idea](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/QffwXEExuGZadwqBK/an-ai-manhattan-project-is-not-inevitable) of a "Manhattan Project for AGI" more seriously after all. I guess we'll wait and see what [Mythos](https://thezvi.substack.com/p/ai-162-visions-of-mythos?open=false#%C2%A7mythos) is all about.
